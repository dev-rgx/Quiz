
      Welcome Chief,
      This Game is filled with plenty of exciting Quizzes that will boost
      your General Knowledge. Several Features are added to it to improve
      its performance and to make appear more realistic.

               # Salient Features:-
               1. You can choose theme - Light or Dark.
               2. You can assign 'Player Name' in Profile Section.
               3. You can analyse your Score in ScoreCard Section.
               4. You can Replay the Game without reopening.

      Before you start the Game. I suggest you to read the following
      instructions for the sake of convenience.

            # Instructions:-
            1. In Quiz, 5 Questions will be asked, each having 4 Options
               denoted by integers i.e., 1, 2, 3 or 4.
            2. Only 1 Option is correct out of the 4 options.
            3. Game does not accept any value or character other than
               1, 2, 3 or 4
            4. Press the Option No. corresponding to your answer.
               (NOTE :- There is no need to press 'Enter' key.)
            5. There is no negative marking.

      It's gonna be fun !
      Good luck! Enjoy the Game......
